About Us

Introducing Rosarium, established in 2024, specializing in roses and flowers for every occasion. With 500 roses grown on Stellenbosch Farm, we offer a stunning array of fresh blooms.
From simple bunches to elaborate arrangements, including wedding flowers, our selection is sure to delight.Visitors are welcome to tour our farm as part of the Wild Coast Jikeleza Route.
We sell directly to the public at Murambi Farm and offer delivery to East London.
Explore our ideas, photos, and catalogues to discover the perfect floral arrangements for you. At Rosarium, we take quality seriously.
